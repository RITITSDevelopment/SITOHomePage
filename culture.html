Mission

SITO provides students with ongoing experiential learning opportunities in the areas of software development, design, project management, and business management. Students are exposed to end-to-end project and application development life cycles.

Vision

To be a fully functioning student IT office that manages, creates, develops, tests, implements, and supports technology to enhance the student experience.
