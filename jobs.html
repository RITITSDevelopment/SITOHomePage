Web Developer

The Student IT Office (SITO) provides the students with a unique coop experience, where students are able to obtain experience and knowledge in software development, project management, and business management. The student is exposed to the end-to-end project and software development lifecycle. SITO encourages work ownership and allows the student to learn while making business decisions. SITO requires web site developers to design, develop, execute, and manage websites and other applications. This includes work on new projects, enhancements of existing student managed projects, and support for existing websites and applications previously developed by the Student IT Office. The web developer will work in teams with a web designer and a business analyst to achieve project success.
